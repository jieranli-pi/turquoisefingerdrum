This project is inspired by yamaha fgdp layout.

Drum kits on your fingertips.

Drumming is fun. It's more fun to play with others!

How to Play:

  • Press a key to hit.
  • Press keys to hit and make sure, the sounds them syncopate with each others.

Basic Key-to-Sound Mappings

  • Space: Kick Drum
  • F, J: snare
  • G, H: harder snare
  • R, U: closed hihat
  • T, Y: open hihat
  • I: ride
  • E: crash
  • S, D, K, L: cymbals
  • Z, X: sticks
  • C, V, B, N, M: toms

Tip: the key-to-sound map layout is symmetrical. One method of playing is to use both hands so you can play the same sound twice fast and steady.
